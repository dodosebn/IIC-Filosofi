Screen Acting START

This screenwriting program explores the foundational elements necessary for creating a compelling and producible screenplay. Before drafting any dialogue, participants will examine the crucial aspects of prewriting and story development. The program highlights the importance of structure, how to create believable characters, and how to construct a vibrant world for their narratives.

In addition to character and plot, this program addresses the often-overlooked practical elements of screenwriting. You will learn industry-standard formatting for screenplays, ensuring your work is polished and professional. By the end of the program, you will possess a solid grasp of the essential technical aspects of screenwriting, empowering you to approach your writing with confidence.

We don’t tell you how or what to write; we teach you everything you need to accomplish before you start writing. Master this craft, and you’ll create scripts you can sell beyond your borders.
