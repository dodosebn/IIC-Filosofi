    Screen Acting START

    Begin your journey in film acting with our comprehensive beginner's program designed to unlock your potential

    Screen Acting PRO | ONE

    GROUP PROGRAM

    The Screen Acting PRO program is designed and taught by Hollywood professional Nayo Wallace. It is an advanced program for experienced actors already working in the industry. The program offers insights into navigating the demands of Hollywood, including working with directors, interpreting scripts, breaking down characters, and collaborating with fellow actors. Practical exercises may include scene work, monologues, improvisation, and more. Some filming may occur for review and to provide constructive feedback. Emphasis would be placed on developing your true voice as an artist, mastering the art of authenticity in front of the lens, and being fully prepared when you arrive on set. The Program is offered to both adults and youth.

    ONE-ON-ONE PROGRAM The Screen Acting ONE is designed and taught by Hollywood professional Nayo Wallace. Advanced private coaching is for experienced actors already working in the industry. Screen Acting ONE is one-on-one coaching tailored for busy professionals who need to enhance their skills. It focuses specifically on areas where the actor needs improvement to elevate their career to the next level. It also includes professional consultation about transitioning into Hollywood's film industry. Screen Acting ONE is available online and in person, providing flexibility for busy actors. It is confidential, and only you and your trainer can access it.

    The Program is offered to both adults and youth.

    The Adult Program is $2000 (US dollars)


    The Youth Program is $1200 (US dollars)


    Participants in both programs will receive a wealth of information on all the aspects of being a working actor in Hollywood. The program will also cover the business of working actors and the emotional resilience required in the industry. It will touch on the value of building a personal brand and understanding your relationship with agents and managers. Additionally, the program will address the psychological challenges of acting and discuss ways to develop and maintain mental health and stamina while navigating the business. By the end of the course, participants would have honed their craft and gained a comprehensive understanding of what it takes to succeed as screen actors on the global stage.
