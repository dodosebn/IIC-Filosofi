Screen Acting START

ONE-ON-ONE PROGRAM The Screen Acting ONE is designed and taught by Hollywood professional Nayo Wallace. Advanced private coaching is for experienced actors already working in the industry. Screen Acting ONE is one-on-one coaching tailored for busy professionals who need to enhance their skills. It focuses specifically on areas where the actor needs improvement to elevate their career to the next level. It also includes professional consultation about transitioning into Hollywood's film industry. Screen Acting ONE is available online and in person, providing flexibility for busy actors. It is confidential, and only you and your trainer can access it.

Acting involves releasing self-consciousness to fully embody a character and provide a raw, emotional performance. It's a journey toward becoming more free, confident, and open to new experiences. The beginner program involves self-paced exercises designed to help actors overcome their inhibitions and become more comfortable. It caters to novices who want to learn correctly from the outset. This represents the first step toward becoming a professional actor.
