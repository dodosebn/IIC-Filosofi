    Screen Acting TOTAL

    The Screen Acting TOTAL program is a comprehensive training course that takes participants from beginners to working actors. It integrates all three levels—beginner, intermediate, and advanced—into one cohesive program. The focus is on preparing actors for successful screen acting careers. This program is specifically tailored for individuals who are mentally committed to pursuing a career in film and are ready to dedicate themselves entirely to screen acting.

    Participants engage in intensive study that requires insight and precision. The program examines natural behavior, sharpens individuality, and enhances innate performances. It incorporates physicality to help you evolve into a multi-dimensional performer. This journey focuses on transcending limitations, embracing vulnerability, and achieving artistry that resonates deeply with the audience.
