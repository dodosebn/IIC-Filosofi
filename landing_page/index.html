BE IN THE SPOTLIGHT

Up your game. Learn from Pros

So you're naturally talented, your uncle is a “big” film producer, and you’re good-looking; then what? Talent alone is not enough! Nepotism (favoritism) does not result in outstanding achievements! Good looks won't sustain a career! None of these states of being will help you in your international career.

Our programs cater to individuals who aspire to work globally. For African film professionals, "local" must evolve into "global." Only those capable and possess the skills, discipline, and dedication will benefit from the 20 million high-paying jobs outlined in the 2021 UNESCO report. Africa is in the spotlight, and companies worldwide are eager to engage with African nations; international film productions are no exception. Foreign casting agents actively seek professionally trained African actors for film and TV projects. Screen actors must be prepared, skilled, and knowledgeable.
