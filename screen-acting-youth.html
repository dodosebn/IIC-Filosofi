Screen Acting Youth

Screen Acting YOUTH is an exciting training program for young performers aged 10 to 15. Participants will explore the realms of film and television while learning the fundamentals of on camera acting. Through various exercises and scene work, they will develop crucial skills such as emotional expression and on-screen presence. The program emphasizes a supportive and encouraging environment where young actors can express their creativity, build confidence, and uncover their unique talents. The program concludes with a showcase, allowing students to present their work and experience a taste of professional acting, laying the foundation for future pursuits in film and television.
