    ABOUT US

    Filosofi embodies the pursuit of wisdom and knowledge, principles that shape our vision for the future of African cinema. Our program trainers are seasoned professionals with an average of 20 years in the international film industry, bringing expertise from various disciplines and countries.

    Our team includes enthusiastic younger members who share the company’s vision of doing things right. Each is knowledgeable in his or her field, believes that Africans can excel, and is on a personal and professional growth journey. They joined the company for its potential, recognizing that Africa has immense untapped opportunities in the film industry. Each understands that this path requires innovative thinking and fresh approaches.
